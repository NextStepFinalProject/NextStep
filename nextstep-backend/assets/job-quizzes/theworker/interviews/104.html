פרטים לגבי התהליך שיחת טלפון ראשונה לתיאום ציפיות. משימת בית.
שאלות מתוך הראיון There is an electrical device, its testing environment and the functionality of its various parts.
o The device is a part of a Radar system.
o The Radar system works with 4 channels.
o The Radar system has a controller unit that selects which channel will be used, it then transfers this information to the device.
o The received channel input is also transferred into the device.
o Acceptance tests are used at the end of the production line. In case the acceptance tests fail, troubleshooting is performed by selecting acceptance or diagnostic tests.
o In the field, the troubleshooting starts by reporting a symptom such as:
No reception on channels 1 and 2, or
No reception on channels 3 and 4.
The troubleshooting is performed by running diagnostic tests, to isolate the faulty assembly.
1. What is the minimal set of tests that will verify the overall functionality of the device?
2. What tests should be defined to isolate all potential faulty components?
